Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a bus ticket?

You can book a ticket by selecting your route, choosing a seat, and making payment through our secure gateway.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 1 hour before departure from the 'My Bookings' section.

What payment options are available?

We support UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, etc.
